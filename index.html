Welcome to Dancing Okra

Where every bite has a beat

Our Story

Dancing Okra is more than just a restaurant; it's a celebration of vibrant flavors, lively music, and warm community. Inspired by the rich culinary traditions of India and the joyful spirit of Bollywood, we aim to create an unforgettable dining experience. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients to craft authentic dishes, while our live Bollywood music nights add a unique and energetic ambiance. Come and experience the magic where every meal is a dance!

Our Delicious Menu

Explore our diverse menu featuring a range of flavorful Indian dishes, from classic curries to tandoori specialties and delightful vegetarian options. Each dish is prepared with passion and authentic spices to tantalize your taste buds.

Okra Curry Special

Our signature dish! Tender okra cooked in a rich and aromatic blend of spices.

Butter Chicken

Classic creamy tomato-based curry with succulent pieces of tandoori chicken.

Vegetable Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with mixed vegetables, herbs, and spices.

Tandoori Mixed Grill

A platter of assorted tandoori meats marinated in yogurt and spices.

Saag Paneer

Creamy spinach and homemade Indian cheese cooked with mild spices.

Garlic Naan

Soft and fluffy Indian bread topped with fresh garlic and cilantro.
